Our Products

Our products are based on naturally procured ingredients that will create an interesting mix of flavor between Japanese and Korean cuisines.

Tteokkochi

Come taste our Tteokkochi. A Korean rice cake skewer with 3 different unique sauces, and a lot of chewiness.

Gimbap

Our Gimbap, Flavored rice covered with seaweed and filled with tons of nutritious yet delicious ingredients such as crabstick, sausages, tuna, Korean pickled radish or known as Danmuji, and many more.

Gyoza

Our Gyoza. Minced-chicken-filled Japanese dumplings mixed with sweet and savory ingredients that complements each other with the texture of its skin.
